Recommended Review Workflow
  1. Open each assigned abstract and read the latest submission version and supplementary files.
  2. Assess the work for quality, clarity, relevance, and feasibility.
  3. Choose one recommendation: approve, request changes, or recommend poster format.
  4. Write comments that explain the decision clearly and give the author something actionable.
  5. Track your pending queue from the reviewer dashboard until all assigned reviews are complete.
Evaluation Criteria
Research Quality & Innovation

Look for originality, sound reasoning, and a credible research contribution.

Relevance to Theme

Check whether the submission fits the selected conference track or theme.

Clarity of Presentation

The abstract should clearly communicate purpose, methods, and results.

Feasibility & Completeness

Assess whether the research is realistic, sufficiently developed, and ready for the festival.
