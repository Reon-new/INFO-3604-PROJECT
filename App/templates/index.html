About Us

The Principal's Research Awards and Research Festival 2025 is a blend of multiple events meant to exhibit outstanding research happening at the UWI, and align it with the needs of policymakers and public and private industries.

On its 65th anniversary, explore these faculties of the St Augustine Campus have embarked to showcase its exciting research as "one mega show" to its internal and external stakeholders connecting diverse disciplinary research that addresses present challenges to build sustainable futures within the theme 'Research Innovation for Present and Future'.

The three-day event will provide a platform for researchers, students, policymakers, development agencies and industry leaders to engage in meaningful discussions about research and development at the UWI, St. Augustine, and its alignment with the National, Regional and United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Research Festival is open to the public to participate and learn more about our ongoing research activities of the St. Augustine campus.
